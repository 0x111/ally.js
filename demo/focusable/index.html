What Elements Are Focusable?

This document is a testing ground to identify what a browser deems focusable and tabbable.

At first the document will add focus event handlers to every descendant of <body>. It will then try to .focus() every element to identify which elements satisfiy Focus Managemenet. After that you - the user - can hit TAB a couple of times (like 50 or something) to determine the browser's natural tabbing order
